# RiotB
This repository is for the development of a practice project called Run It On The Beat, or RiotB for short.

## What is RiotB?
RiotB is a program planned to gather songs through various APIs and group them for the user based on either genre, rhythm measured by BPM, or both.

## Why make RiotB?
Have you ever experienced that feeling of walking while listening to one of your favorite songs, perhaps in a rush or strolling along, and your steps coincide to the rhythm of the song? If you have, you know that it adds a little extra oomph to your step and a little extra groove to your music. If you haven't, I hope this project would make experiencing it with your favorite songs easier! For a long time, I have enjoyed fitness and getting into that mental state where you flow with the movement and lock into a rhythm for your exercise. While some call music a distraction in the gym that prevents you from truly focusing, the right song for many others and myself can push you through that last stretch of a run or the reps of your last set. There are many pre-made playlists available on popular music streaming services, but these are often songs oriented towards fitness with high BPM rhythms or a selection made by someone else. The end-goal of this project would be allowing creation of a playlist consisting of desired songs within a specified genre and/or BPM range, which could then be exported to a popular music streaming service.

### What is next?
- Develop construction and style of site further
- Research and document necessary APIs
- Build filtering systems based on either genre, BPM, or both (perhaps more at a later time)
- Determine acceptable variance of selected BPM
- Add evenly-factoring BPM values to results
  - (i.e. Cadence matching 120 BPM would allow 1 step per 1 beat. Song with 60 BPM would allow 2 steps per 1 beat. Etc.)
- Return songs matching filtered genre and/or BPM
- Research ability to store song values into a playlist
- Research ability to export saved playlist to a major streaming platform
